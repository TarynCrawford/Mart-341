1. What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?
 IP addresses are found by your computer when looking up websites using DNS, where the DNS allows your computer to connect
   to another through its IP address.

2. What is a cookie?
A cookie is a bit of data a website sends to your computer to allow the website to remember who you are.

3. List the three basic parts of a URL.
The three basic parts of a URL are; the protocol (http://), the domain (.com), and the path (/directory/).
