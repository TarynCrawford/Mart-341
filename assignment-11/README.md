1.  Typography is the design and technique of making appealing, legible, and well-arranged type that will add interest to its medium. 
2. A fallback font is important because it is used when the primary font face has not loaded yet or is unable to load due to a browser incompatibility or poor network connection.
3. System Fonts are already installed on your local device and are available to you and your programs but often have licensing that makes them unavailable on the web. Web fonts are custom fonts that are hosted by a server. They are optimized for web performance, but browser compatibility and speeds can affect them. Web-safe fonts are limited, but they can be used on both Windows and Mac. Every computer will already have the font to it makes load times faster.
